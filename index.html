Frequently Asked Questions

What safety measures do you have?

Rest assured, your safety is our top priority. Our team of experienced astronauts and engineers conduct rigorous testing and follow strict safety protocols to ensure a secure, enjoyable space adventure.

Do I need any special training?

Our tours are designed for everyone! Prior to your space adventure, you’ll undergo a fun, interactive training program that’ll get you ready for your cosmic journey.

Can children join the space tours?

Absolutely! Space Tour Adventures welcomes junior astronauts aged 10 and above, accompanied by a parent or guardian. Unleash their inner astronaut for an unparalleled experience!

What if I change my mind?

No worries! We understand the uncertainty that comes with planning a cosmic adventure. Our flexible cancellation policy allows you to reschedule or cancel your trip up to 30 days before departure with a full refund.
